# Open-Ended Questions
It is also known as **Calibrated Questions**

## Key Elements of this Technique:

**1. No Fixed Answers**

- Questions that can't be answered with simple "yes/no"
- Requires thoughtful, detailed responses

**2. Illusion of Control**

- Makes the respondent feel they're in charge
- They believe they're providing valuable information
- Creates psychological comfort

**3. Time Advantage**

- Buys thinking time for the questioner
- Forces the other party to engage deeply

**4. Information Gathering**

- Reveals the other side's perspective
- Uncovers hidden motivations/thoughts

**5. Subtle Control**

- While appearing to give power to respondent
- Actually constrains their thinking within questioner's framework
- They don't realize they're being guided
