# 🗓️ **FULL-BODY WEEKLY TRAINING PLAN**

You will train **6 days / week**  
Each session: **60–90 minutes**

---

## **MONDAY — Power + Strength**

_Build muscle, bone density & testosterone_

**Warm-up (10 min)**

- Jump rope – 3 min
    
- Mobility drills (hips, shoulders)
    
- Light jogging – 5 min
    

**Strength (3–4 rounds)**

- Push-ups × 15
    
- Squats × 20
    
- Lunges × 12 each leg
    
- Resistance band rows × 15
    
- Dumbbell or barbell shoulder press × 10
    

**Power Finisher**

- Boxing (heavy bag or shadowboxing) – 10 min
    

**Cool down**

- Stretching + yoga – 10 min
    

---

## **TUESDAY — Cardio Endurance**

_Heart = longer life_

Choose **ONE main cardio**:

- Jogging OR
    
- Cycling OR
    
- Swimming OR
    
- Rowing
    

Do **30–45 minutes**

Then:

- Walking – 15 min
    
- Core balance work – 10 min
    
- Stretching – 10 min
    

---

## **WEDNESDAY — Athletic & Balance**

_Prevents injury and keeps you young_

**Warm-up**

- Dancing – 10 min
    

**Main work**

- Basketball – 30–45 min
    
- Stability ball exercises – 10 min
    
- Standing on one leg (eyes closed) – 5 min
    

**Cool down**

- Tai Chi – 10 min
    
- Stretching – 10 min
    

---

## **THURSDAY — Strength + Body Control**

_Strong muscles = longer lifespan_

**Strength Circuit (4 rounds)**

- Push-ups × 15
    
- Weight lifting (deadlift or squat) × 8
    
- Resistance band chest press × 15
    
- Pull-ups or rows × 8–10
    
- Plank – 60 sec
    

**Finish**

- Jump rope – 10 min
    

**Cool down**

- Yoga + mobility – 15 min
    

---

## **FRIDAY — Speed, Power & Fighting**

_Explosive strength = anti-aging_

**Warm-up**

- Jogging – 10 min
    

**Main**

- Boxing – 30 min
    
- Bodyweight HIIT (burpees, squats, mountain climbers) – 15 min
    

**Balance & Core**

- Stability ball
    
- One-leg exercises
    
- Core work – 15 min
    

---

## **SATURDAY — Nature & Recovery**

_Longevity secret: move gently, outside_

- Hiking OR long walk – 60+ minutes
    
- Light cycling or swimming – optional
    
- Pilates + yoga – 30 min
    

---

## **SUNDAY — Rest or Light Movement**

- Walking
    
- Stretching
    
- Tai Chi
    

---

# 🧠 Why This Makes You Live Longer

This plan hits the **5 pillars of longevity**:

|Pillar|You’re training it|
|---|---|
|Heart health|Running, cycling, swimming, hiking|
|Muscle & bones|Weight lifting, pushups, squats|
|Brain & coordination|Basketball, boxing, dancing|
|Joints & flexibility|Yoga, Pilates, mobility|
|Balance|Tai Chi, one-leg, stability ball|

This combination is what **Blue Zone people** and elite athletes do.

- Maximize testosterone
